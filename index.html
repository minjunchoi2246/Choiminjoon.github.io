About Me

Stories shaped by reverence, contradiction, and human presence.

A filmmaker drawn to unidealized humanity, subtle emotional tension, and the quiet complexity hidden inside ordinary lives.

Driven by a profound reverence for life and humanity, I make films to explore the deeper, unidealized truths of who we are. My strength lies in crafting nuanced original screenwrites and capturing raw, multi-dimensional human stories through deliberate documentary filmmaking. For me, the camera is a thoughtful perspective used to honor the depth of my subjects and bring unvarnished human existence to the screen.
